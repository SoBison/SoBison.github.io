üîê Privacy Commitment: iBooks is a privacy-focused application. We adopt a local-first design philosophy. Your reading data is stored locally on your device by default and will not be uploaded to any server without your consent.

1. Information Collection

The types of information we collect are as follows:

Information Type Collection Method Purpose
Reading Data Automatic (Local Storage) Record reading progress and bookmarks
Reading Statistics Automatic (Local Storage) Generate reading time and habit analysis
Purchase Information Via App Store Verify membership status
WebDAV Credentials User Provided Enable cloud sync functionality

2. Information Use

We use the collected information for:

  • Core Services: E-book reading and progress tracking
  • Data Sync: When you enable WebDAV sync, data is synchronized to your designated server
  • Statistical Analysis: Generate reading statistics reports locally
  • Membership Verification: Verify your purchase status

3. Information Storage

  • Local Storage: Your reading data and settings are stored locally on your device by default
  • Cloud Storage: Data is only synchronized to your designated server when you actively configure and enable WebDAV sync
  • Encryption Protection: Sensitive information (such as WebDAV passwords) is stored securely

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Information is only shared in the following circumstances:

  • User-Initiated: When you configure WebDAV sync, data is sent to your designated server
  • Legal Requirements: As required by law or mandatory government requests

5. Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store: Process in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • WebDAV Services: Cloud storage services configured by you
  • OPDS Book Sources: Online book sources added by you

These third-party services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reading them carefully before use.

6. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

Privacy information regarding in-app purchases and subscriptions:

  • Purchase information is processed by Apple / Google; we only receive purchase verification results
  • We do not store your payment information (such as credit card numbers)
  • Subscription status is cached locally to provide offline experience

7. Data Security

We take the following measures to protect your data security:

  • Sensitive credentials are stored using system secure storage (Keychain / Keystore)
  • Local database encrypted storage
  • Network transmission uses HTTPS encryption

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: You can view data stored in the App at any time
  • Right to Delete: You can delete reading records, books, and other data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can disable WebDAV sync or delete configurations at any time

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you discover that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to delete the information.

10. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated policies will be posted within the App or on the website. Continued use of the App indicates your agreement to the updated Privacy Policy.
