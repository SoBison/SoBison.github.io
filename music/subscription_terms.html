3. Payment & Billing

  • All payments are processed through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store ("Platform").
  • Prices are displayed in your local currency as determined by the Platform. Applicable taxes may apply.
  • Payment is charged to your Platform account upon confirmation of purchase.

4. Auto-Renewal

  • Subscriptions automatically renew at the end of each billing period (monthly or yearly) unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
  • Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period, at the same price as the original subscription.
  • Price changes, if any, will be communicated in advance and will take effect at the next renewal cycle with your consent where required by law.

5. Free Trial

If a free trial is offered, you will not be charged during the trial period. At the end of the trial, your subscription will automatically convert to a paid subscription unless you cancel before the trial ends. Any unused portion of a free trial will be forfeited upon purchasing a subscription.

6. Cancellation

  • You may cancel your subscription at any time through your Platform account settings (Apple: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions; Google: Play Store → Subscriptions).
  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. You will retain access to subscription features until that date.
  • Cancellation does not entitle you to a refund of the current billing period.

7. Refunds

Refund requests are handled by the Platform in accordance with their refund policies:

  • Apple: Request a refund from Apple
  • Google: Request a refund from Google Play

8. Upgrades & Downgrades

  • You may upgrade from Prism to Aurora at any time. The Platform will prorate or credit the remaining balance of your current plan.
  • You may switch between monthly and yearly billing within the same tier at any time.
  • Downgrade from Aurora to Prism takes effect at the end of the current billing period.
  • Supernova (lifetime) is a one-time purchase. If you have an active subscription, it will continue until the current period ends; we recommend canceling the subscription after purchasing Supernova to avoid duplicate charges.

9. Lifetime Purchase (Supernova)

  • The Supernova plan is a one-time, non-refundable purchase that grants lifetime access to all Aurora-tier features.
  • "Lifetime" means for the operational life of the App. Should the App be permanently discontinued, we will provide at least 12 months' notice.

10. Changes to Plans & Pricing

We reserve the right to modify subscription plans, features, or pricing at any time. Existing subscribers will be notified before any price increase takes effect for their next renewal.
